Reference

This page explains how The European Manifesto and its supporting book, The European House, may be referenced and used.

The European Manifesto

Reference
The European Manifesto. https://europeanmanifesto.eu

Use
The European Manifesto may be used freely and without restriction. It may be copied, shared, reproduced, adapted, translated, incorporated into other works, and used for any purpose, including political or commercial use, without permission and without attribution.

The European House

Reference
Loterman, G. (2026). The European House: What’s Wrong — And How to Fix It. ISBN 979-8-244364-70-5.

Use
The European House is protected by copyright. Short quotations and references are permitted with attribution. All other rights are reserved.

Discussion

The ideas presented in both The European Manifesto and The European House are not presented as doctrine. They are intended to be tested, challenged, debated, and refined as conditions for institutional change.
