The European Manifesto

Three Rules for a Europe That Can Act

Download the manifesto (PDF)

Introduction

We pay for a Europe that cannot act.

When action is needed, no one is in charge.
When something goes wrong, everyone is to blame.
And when the bill arrives, we pay.

We therefore insist on the minimum conditions for Europe to work.

Rule 1 — We Choose

We directly choose who holds European power.

One equal vote for a president who chooses and leads the executive.
One equal vote for representatives who shape the laws.
The candidates with the most votes win.

Rule 2 — We Empower

We directly fund the power we choose.

One shared contribution.
No intermediate taxation layers.
No additional taxation.

Rule 3 — We Oversee

We directly oversee the power we choose and fund.

Citizens chosen by lot watch the system.
All shared decisions are public.
Every income and expense is transparent.

Call to Action

We endorse political actors only if they put these three rules first.

Above parties.
Above ideologies.
Above policy goals within the current system.

Anyone who puts anything else first guarantees dysfunction and waste.
At our expense.
